The Shadow of the Wind

Author: Carlos Ruiz Zafón

The Shadow of the Wind by Carlos Ruiz Zafón is a captivating mystery novel set in post-war Barcelona. The story follows young Daniel Sempere as he discovers a hidden library of forgotten books, leading him into a web of secrets, intrigue, and romance. As Daniel delves into the enigmatic life of the mysterious author, Julian Carax, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat-and-mouse with a sinister figure known only as "The Keeper of the Cemetery." With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot twists, The Shadow of the Wind is a literary masterpiece that will keep readers eagerly turning pages until the very end.
